Without our sponsors, we would not be able to host and run such a fantastic tournament every year. We’d like to express our overwhelming gratitude to those that have helped make this competition possible. Below is a list of our sponsors categorized by their level of sponsorship. If you’re interested in sponsoring or donating to our organization, please email us at scienceolympiadvt@gmail.com.

Science Olympiad at Virginia Tech (SO@VT) is a non-profit student-run chapter of Virginia Science Olympiad (VASO) that plans an annual Science Olympiad Regionals tournament for middle and high school teams throughout Virginia. Our team is committed to giving all students access to higher level STEM education in a competitive environment.
