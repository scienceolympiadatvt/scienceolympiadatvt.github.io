Join the Team

The SO@VT team is student-run organization committed to making a difference in the STEM community and to giving younger generations access to opportunities that will jump start their higher education. We accept applications to join the SO@VT team during the spring semester. It is highly encouraged that you volunteer at this year’s tournament to be a more competitive applicant in spring; check out the Volunteer page for more info. If you’re interested in becoming a part of our team, please email us at scienceolympiadvt@gmail.com.

What's SO@VT all about?

Science Olympiad at Virginia Tech (SO@VT) is a non-profit student-run chapter of Virginia Science Olympiad (VASO) that plans an annual Science Olympiad Regionals tournament for middle and high school teams throughout Virginia. Our team is committed to giving all students access to higher level STEM education in a competitive environment.
