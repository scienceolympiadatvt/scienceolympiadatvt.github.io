Volunteer

SO@VT Regionals would be utterly impossible without the amazing work of our volunteers each tournament. We have a few hundred volunteers come out each year to help with running events, scoring events, handing out awards, and just helping students get around campus. If you need volunteer hours, this is the perfect place to come. Our tournament lasts all day, so you can knock out a bunch of your volunteer hours at once. What's SO@VT all about?

Science Olympiad at Virginia Tech (SO@VT) is a non-profit student-run chapter of Virginia Science Olympiad (VASO) that plans an annual Science Olympiad Regionals tournament for middle and high school teams throughout Virginia. Our team is committed to giving all students access to higher level STEM education in a competitive environment.
