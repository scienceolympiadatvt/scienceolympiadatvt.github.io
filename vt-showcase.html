VT Showcase

The Virginia Tech Showcase is an hour-long event that demonstrates some of the most exciting interesting clubs and even colleges found at Virginia Tech. This gives Science Olympiad middle and high school students a chance to check out what life at Virginia Tech is like. It also gives the selected clubs and colleges an opportunity to recruit the next generation of bright students. We highly encourage your students to come check out the booths and pick up some free swag during their time at the Science Olympiad at Virginia Tech Regionals tournament!

What's SO@VT all about?

Science Olympiad at Virginia Tech (SO@VT) is a non-profit student-run chapter of Virginia Science Olympiad (VASO) that plans an annual Science Olympiad Regionals tournament for middle and high school teams throughout Virginia. Our team is committed to giving all students access to higher level STEM education in a competitive environment.
