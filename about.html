What is Science Olympiad?

Science Olympiad is a nationwide organization that promotes life science, earth science, chemistry, engineering, and inquiry-based education. Annually, this organization hosts about 300 regional and state tournaments across the United States. Students from elementary through high school are able to compete and represent their school in teams of 15. These tournaments function much like track meets, in that each student trains for a few events that they compete in the day of the tournament. These events consist of 23 challenging testing and engineering events. Students are eligible to earn individual awards with medals going to the top six in each event, as well as teams are given awards based on their performance overall.

Our Mission

The Science Olympiad at Virginia Tech organization is committed to giving all students access to higher level STEM education in a competitive environment. Our current outreach goals include aiding in the formation of Science Olympiad teams in the Southwest Virginia area to further out-of-classroom learning as well as motivating students of all races, ethnicities, and identities to participate in this wonderful experience.

Subject Areas

Life and Earth Science

Examples of life and earth science events are Anatomy & Physiology, Disease Detectives, Ornithology, Dynamic Planet, Water Quality, Fossils, and Reach for the Starts/Astronomy.

Chemistry

Events that involve chemistry are Chem Lab, Crime Busters/Forensics, and Food Science/Protein Modeling.

Engineering

Engineering events include Boomilever, Elastic Launch Glider/Wright Stuff, Mission Possible/Detector Building, and Mousetrap Vehicle/Gravity Vehicle.

Inquiry

Events that are inquiry-based include Experimental Design, Game On/Code Busters, Ping-Pong Parachute, and WIDI.

What's SO@VT all about?

Science Olympiad at Virginia Tech (SO@VT) is a non-profit student-run chapter of Virginia Science Olympiad (VASO) that plans an annual Science Olympiad Regionals tournament for middle and high school teams throughout Virginia. Our team is committed to giving all students access to higher level STEM education in a competitive environment.
